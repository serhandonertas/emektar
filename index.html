ABOUT US

As EMEKTAR MAKNA, we have been providing machining solutions to the automotive, mold, aerospace, medical and defense industry sectors since 1956, with our experienced staff; We bring our customers together with high technology, together with sales and service and application support in CNC machine tools, universal machines, sheet metal working machines.

The world of machine tools has become globalized. It takes strong alliances to overcome challenges. We have become one of the leading companies in the sector by combining our strengths, knowledge and experience with our producer and importer companies.

VETERAN MACHINING MOBILE APP

WHAT DOES IT DO?
The Veteran Machining mobile application is a guide for quickly obtaining calculations and basic information for the manufacturing sector, which constitutes an important part of the industrialization in our country.
The Veteran Machining application has been developed to quickly answer the problems that may be encountered during manufacturing from your mobile phone. Within the application; Geometric Tolerances, Insertion Tolerances, Surface Tolerances, Unit Converter, G and M codes, Material Hardness Tables, Drill Speed Calculation, Milling Calculation, Guide Calculations, Guide Tables, Machinability Performances of Materials, Slope and Arc Calculation, Thermal Expansion Calculation, SFM Converter, Thanks to many modules such as Machining Roughness Table, Wedge Dimensions, Material Codes, all literature information about manufacturing engineering is gathered under a single roof. With the artificial intelligence module that will come soon, it will enable the technical personnel who have just started in the manufacturing sector to integrate into the sector earlier and reduce the time to get used to the job to a minimum.
